About

João Dias-Oliveira

Designer and Engineer

Currently collaborating as a freelancer in design projects with many scales and natures. These range from immaterial projects for communication and web-design, to physical projects with development of artistic installations, products and architectural interventions. Other technical services are also offered, such as structural analysis. This working strategy strives for a merge of a passion and sensibility for artistic/conceptual problems with technical skills of engineering and programming. A particular interest of work is linked with the preservation of built patrimony and social impact projects.
